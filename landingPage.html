👨‍⚕️ Doctor Management

The Doctor Management system allows you to:

  • Add and update doctor profiles
  • Specify medical specializations
  • Manage availability and schedules
  • View doctor ratings and reviews
  • Assign patients to doctors

🧑‍⚕️ Patient Management

The Patient Management system allows you to:

  • Register new patients
  • Maintain complete medical records
  • Track medical history and diagnoses
  • Store emergency contact information
  • Monitor patient health status

💰 Billing & Payments

The Billing system allows you to:

  • Generate detailed invoices
  • Track payment transactions
  • Manage insurance claims
  • Generate financial reports
  • Process refunds and adjustments

💊 Treatment Plans

The Treatment Management system allows you to:

  • Create personalized treatment plans
  • Prescribe medications
  • Schedule therapy sessions
  • Track patient progress
  • Update treatment status

📅 Appointments

The Appointment Management system allows you to:

  • Schedule doctor-patient appointments
  • Send appointment reminders
  • Manage appointment cancellations
  • View appointment history
  • Optimize doctor schedules
